**6. Austin Multifamily Supply Pipeline May Pressure Rent Growth**
Austin continues to experience elevated multifamily deliveries, which could suppress market rent growth and limit the achievable premium for renovated units. *Mitigation:* The subject property's submarket has below-average new supply, and the targeted $150/month rent bump positions renovated units below comparable new construction, preserving relative value.
