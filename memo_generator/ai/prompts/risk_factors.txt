Write the RISK FACTORS section of the Investment Memorandum.

For each risk identified, provide:
1. A clear risk statement (bold)
2. One sentence explaining the nature of the risk
3. One sentence on the mitigation strategy

Also include 1-2 macro/systematic risks (interest rate environment, cap rate expansion) even if not explicitly listed.

Format as a structured list. Be honest about risks — sophisticated investors expect candor, not spin.
